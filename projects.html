Autonomous Claw Retrieval System – Claw Design & Programming

Skills Used: C++ Programming • Arduino & Electronics • SolidWorks CAD • Engineering Drawings • Team Collaboration

As part of a design project for APSC 100/101 at UBC, our team developed an autonomous claw mechanism to compete in a class-wide claw retrieval challenge.

Project Overview

The goal was to design, build, and program a functional robotic claw that could autonomously retrieve both varied and standardized objects within timed rounds of a competitive event. Our solution integrated C++ code on an Arduino microcontroller, a sonar sensor for object detection, and a servo motor for actuation. Our team also prepared formal engineering drawings.

My Role:

  • Successfully integrated sonar sensing and servo actuation
  • Gained experience in interdisciplinary teamwork and physical prototyping

Rainwater Harvesting System - Excel Simulation Specialist

Skills Used: Excel Modeling • Team Collaboration • Stakeholder Assessment

For my intro to Engineering course in first-year engineering at UBC, I collaborated with a team of peers to design a decentralized rainwater harvesting (RWH) system tailored for a two-person household in a remote northern BC community—mirroring the real challenges faced by many small island and rural settlements in the region.

Project Overview

Our mission was to select 30 interconnected parameters to create a self-sufficient system that collects rainwater, purifies it for safe use, stores it for daily needs, runs independently using off-grid power, and is designed to meet key stakeholder priorities like reliability, cost, sustainability, and ease of maintenance. We analyzed interviews with representatives from a Water Improvement District, shaping evaluation metrics like user satisfaction, emissions, cost, and ease of service.

My Role:

I developed a multi-sheet Excel model (10+ interconnected sheets) that simulated:

  • Rainfall-based water availability
  • Tank dynamics (filling, overflow, supply)
  • Household demand coverage
  • Component performance (filters, pumps, power, disinfection)
  • Stakeholder satisfaction scoring

Outcome & Team Achievements

  • Placed 1st out of 10 teams in our studio
  • Passed all project requirements
  • Achieved 91.93% stakeholder satisfaction
  • Delivered a narrated e-poster summarizing our results
